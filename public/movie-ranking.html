Best 3 Movies of all time according to Juan


Hi! Here are my top 3 movies of all time!

1. The Godfather

Shit, what's not to like! A grasping tale of power and family survival, where one man's antihero journey, is a war for his very soul.

2. Forrest Gump

An absolute masterpiece: should I have a single movie for all my life, this would be a strong contender.

3. Akira

An absolute masterpiece, deep in cyberpunk lore. Mesmerizing to watch!
